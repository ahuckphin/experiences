Car Electrical Wiring Repair

The car I worked on previously had an aftermarket alarm system installed which was spliced into the car's original wiring harness at various different points. Wishing to following components were either entirely uninstalled or partially disassmbled:

front 2 seats

dashboard

driver side skirting

front driver side front fender

front bumper

front driver door

For wires which was cut, the wires were rejoined with solder and covered with heat shrink.

For wires which only had portion of the insulation missing, the wires were depinned from the connector allowing me to slide a piece of heat shrink over the exposed portion of the wire.
